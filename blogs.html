Neha Kakkar

Neha Kakkar is one of Bollywood's most well-known singers. She began her career in 2006. She began her career with Meet Bros, releasing her first album, 'Neha The RockStar' in 2008 and immediately rose to prominence in the Bollywood music business.She has performed in almost 1000 live concerts and When it comes to her singing concerts, the figures are staggering.

Ari Staprans Leff

Ari Staprans Leff professionally known as Lauv, is an American singer, songwriter and record producer based in Los Angeles. His debut EP Lost in the Light was released in 2015 and his compilation album I Met You When I Was 18 (The Playlist) was released in 2018. He has written songs for other artists including the singles "No Promises" by Cheat Codes and Demi Lovato and more.

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins is an English pop singer and songwriter whose soulful emotive voice and traditionally crafted songs made her one of the most broadly popular performers of her generation.In 2013 Adele won the Grammy for best pop solo performance for "Set Fire to the Rain" from her concert album Live at the Royal Albert Hall.
